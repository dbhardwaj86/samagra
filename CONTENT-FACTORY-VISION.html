Strategy memo · 2026-06-23 · Chairman → CEO · Carte blanche

From a read-only surface to a physics content factory

A reimagining of SAMAGRA: stop being a window onto the content operation and become the line that manufactures it — turning one physics idea (a lecture, a question, a captured doubt) into a wide spread of catalogued, indexed, style-true student content, with the human publish gate as the one seam that never automates.

The one-sentence thesis

Build a style-conditioned, multi-output factory line steered by a coverage graph: generalize the proven bridge into a guarded dispatch boundary that fans one approved seed into N content types, condition every generated draft on a durable profile of Deepak's voice, and let a concept-coverage matrix compute what to build next — all behind the never-automated publish gate.

This memo is grounded in a 16-agent exploration of the live codebase (11 subsystem maps), a four-angle reimagining, and an adjudication pass. The headline finding reframes the whole exercise: your carte blanche is not a teardown of the safety architecture — it is the activation of machinery the 2026-06-19 vision deliberately designed and parked.

The latent capability

The bridge already does, for one output, exactly what a factory does for many: it classifies a raw input, resolves cross-corpus pointers, proposes, gates on a human, and writes exactly once — crash-safe. The factory is the bridge, widened.

2The reframe — activation, not teardown

The instinct behind "convert the read-only surface into a factory" is to tear down the read-only firewall (DEC-1/DEC-3). That instinct is wrong, and the project's own design history says why. The 2026-06-19 evolution spec pre-designed the content-factory machinery and left it dormant:

① The separate twin (A6)

"If a learner-facing product ever emerges, it is a SEPARATE entity that consumes SAMAGRA's published corpus… the published OUTPUT MAY be public-facing via that separate entity."

→ The student surface is permitted by the vision, not a violation. It consumes the corpus; the console stays inward.

② The DRAFTER & REVIEWER

A drafter that "may compound on Deepak's approval history (style/selection/structure)"; one adversarial reviewer anchored only to external ground-truth, never trained on his approvals — "catch-rate trending to zero is a RED FLAG (monoculture)."

→ Style replication + a quality moat, already specified.

③ The demand compass

Steer generation by which concepts the QX paper-builder under-draws and by post-use flags ("too hard", "students missed this").

→ Coverage-driven prioritization, already named.

So the pivot is: activate (1)+(2)+(3), and generalize the single bridge write into a many-output dispatch — while the read-only firewall over the seven source subsystems stays exactly as hardened. This is a far stronger, lower-risk move than relaxing the invariant the whole architecture rests on.

3The reimagined factory

Three layers, each grafted from the best of a competing reimagining, each touching no binding invariant:

The three-layer factory. Steering decides what; the engine manufactures many from one; the moat makes it sound like Deepak; the publish gate stays human on every lane. PRATHAM is the eventual outward consumer, not the first build.

3.1 · Engine — the dispatch spine

Generalize bridge.submit (today: one write to mcd) into samagra/bridge/dispatch.py — a single guarded write-boundary that routes by pipeline to an existing renderer. One approved seed fans into N child assignments, each running the identical scan → propose → board-approve → single-write → captured discipline with all five crash-safety guards intact. No engine bypasses dispatch.

# the structural heart — every lane shares one guarded boundary
ENGINES = {
  "revision":  lambda seed, ptr: thin.build_thin(load(ptr.chapter)),      # deterministic
  "paper":     lambda seed, ptr: qx.assemble(ptr.qids, variant="student"), # answer-safe
  "lecture":   lambda seed, ptr: lectures.export(ptr.chapter, "both"),     # HTML/DOCX/GDoc
  "audio":     lambda seed, ptr: notebooklm.studio_create("audio", ...),   # async-pending
  "figure":    lambda seed, ptr: imagegen(seed.brief),                     # async-pending
  "seed":      lambda seed, ptr: McdClient().create_seed(payload),         # today's path
}
def dispatch(assignment):           # ← inherits bridge's 5 guards verbatim
    require_approved(assignment); refuse_double_write(assignment)
    validate_output(assignment.payload)        # answer-leak guard at the boundary
    record_intent(assignment)                  # crash-window safe (seed_submitting style)
    art = ENGINES[assignment.pipeline](assignment.seed, assignment.pointers)
    record_created(assignment, art); set_status(assignment, "captured")

This activates the five already-decorative pipelines in state.py (textbook / mycontentdev / questions / papers / media) as actual factory lanes. Async engines (NotebookLM, image-gen) get a distinct pending state with their own intent-before-dispatch guard — the one genuinely new robustness problem (§9).

3.2 · Moat — the StyleSeed

Insert a style layer between resolve and propose. The StyleSeed is a versioned, owner-curated, durable artifact (parallel to governance.db, never reset) with five facets extracted from the 59 chapters' verbatim voice:

  • Voice rubric — idiom, register, the "a gift appears" / "trap" turns of phrase the corpus genuinely carries.
  • Sequencing patterns — how he orders intuition → equation → caveat.
  • Analogy library — the recurring physical pictures.
  • Rigor stance — mined from the flags[] the content.json already encodes.
  • Problem-selection priors — what he draws, at what difficulty mix.

Deterministic lanes (thin / QX) carry his voice for free (the source IS his words). Only the LLM lanes (NotebookLM script, net-new prose) need conditioning — which bounds the compute cost. An advisory style-fit score surfaces on the board, hard-wired to never auto-advance the gate. An owner-ratified learning loop mines review_overlay edit-diffs into profile-deltas the human explicitly promotes (mirrors approve/submit) — finally wiring the dormant thin.py LLM hook and the spec's reserved decision-ledger inference. The single adversarial reviewer stays anchored only to external ground-truth; catch-rate → 0 is a red flag, not success.

3.3 · Steering — the coverage graph

Promote the bridge's throwaway "up to 5 pointers" into a first-class, rebuildable concept_graph.db (sibling to catalog.db, read-only-derived — never mistaken for a governance reset). Nodes = concepts + artifacts (by uid, not duplicated); typed edges (teaches, tests, visualizes, prerequisite_of, similar_to) built by three engines already in the repo: FTS5 (free, structural), QX BGE (semantic), graphify (inferred, flagged, cached). The coverage matrix — concept × content-type — turns "what should exist next" from a guess into a computable, ranked, pointer-pre-loaded worklist. Empty cells become proposed gap-seeds (the human still approves every one).

Each phase ends green (TDD, the project's standing discipline) and the risk surface — new write boundaries — gets a Codex pre-merge review exactly as the Phase-3 bridge did.

One-time style extraction over the 59-chapter corpus → a v0 StyleSeed (voice + analogies + sequencing + rigor from flags[] + selection priors), hand-reviewed by Deepak, calibrated on circular-motion as the gold exemplar. Ships an artifact before any pipeline exists.

B

Dispatch spine TDD · stub engine

Land samagra/bridge/dispatch.py + the additive governance migration (assignments.engine, parent_seed_ref). Test first: a generalized submit routes by pipeline and still enforces all 5 guards + the answer-leak write-boundary assertion. Codex pre-merge review of the new boundaries.

C

First real lanes = the golden thread deterministic, no external auth

Wire the QX drill/paper lane end-to-end (seed → resolve qx:* → approve → dispatch → student paper, zero answers asserted → publish). Add the revision/lecture lane (thin + export). One seed produces a real drill pack through the identical loop — these two carry his voice for free.

D

Style conditioning on the LLM lanes TDD · all pre-gate

Wire the thin.py caller seam to samagra/style/generate.py behind a flag; regenerate one chapter style-conditioned vs the baseline; capture Deepak's first edit-diff. Add the advisory style-fit scorer (never auto-advances). Ratify DEC-7/DEC-8.

E

Coverage graph as the demand engine read-only first

Build concept_graph.db Tier-1 structural edges (QX question_concept + textbook teaches-edges + mcd NCERT aliases); ship GET /api/graph/coverage + a read-only Concept Atlas. Prove fan-out on "Gauss's Law." Then let the coverage scan propose gap-cell seeds (human still approves each).

F

Async LLM lanes + learning-loop maturity new robustness work

Wire NotebookLM (audio/slides) + image-gen (figures) with their distinct async-pending crash-window state; activate the style learning loop (edit-diffs → owner-ratified profile-deltas); add Tier-2 (BGE) + Tier-3 (graphify, cached) edges with review-overlay curation.

G

PRATHAM student twin deferred · Chairman-gated

Only after the inward factory is proven and attention-ROI is positive, and only on an explicit re-scope of DEC-1: the separate student surface (A6 carve-out) — Saar sheets first, a new multi-tenant identity subsystem, a single outward POST /api/factory/publish, batch-by-chapter approval to defend the gate.

→ Proposed amendments

DEC-7 The bridge generalizes from "one mcd write" to "one guarded dispatch boundary fanning to N engines, each board-approved + single-write." An extension that strengthens the firewall — but a wording change to a binding decision, so it needs a Codex pre-merge review of the new boundaries.

DEC-8 The StyleSeed is a durable, versioned, owner-curated governance artifact (never reset). Style-fit scoring is advisory, hard-wired never to auto-advance. The learning loop is owner-ratified-only.

DEC-9 PRATHAM (A6's separate entity) is the eventual outward destination; DEC-1's "no audience/users" clause is scoped to the console only. Deferred until the inward factory is proven.
